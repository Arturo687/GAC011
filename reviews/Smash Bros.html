Smash Bros Ultimate

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is a crossover fighting video game in the Super Smash Bros. series developed by Bandai Namco Games and Sora Ltd. and published by Nintendo.

What is it about?

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, like other video games in the Super Smash Bros. series, features a crossover of fighters from different Nintendo franchises - such as the Mario and Metroid video game series - as well as iconic fighters from third-party properties, such as Sonic. the Hedgehog, Pac-Man, Solid Snake, and Mega Man. Ultimate includes all characters from previous Super Smash Bros. titles, including those offered through downloadable content in Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS and Wii U. Plus , the concept of echo characters was introduced, characters that were previously based on a fighter and only changed their aesthetic appearance and some minor changes to their stats or move sets. These characters are treated as unique characters within the Ultimate fighter roster. For example, Daisy from the Super Mario franchise is an individual echo character in Ultimate (previously she was only depicted as a change in Peach's color palette). New fighters for this installment include Splatoon's Inklings; Ridley and Dark Samus, from Metroid; Simon and Richter Belmont of Castlevania; Chrom from Fire Emblem; Donkey Kong Country's King K.
