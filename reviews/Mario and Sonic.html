Mario & Sonic at the Olympic Games Tokyo 2020

Mario & Sonic at the Olympic Games Tokyo 2020 is a 2019 sports video game based on the Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games. It is a crossover between the Nintendo-owned Mario franchise and the Sega-owned Sonic the Hedgehog franchise.

What is it about?

Like the previous Mario and Sonic at the Olympic Games titles, Mario and Sonic at the Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games is a sports video game that features a crossover between characters from the Mario and Sonic the Hedgehog franchises, owned by Nintendo and Sega respectively. The player, using one of the characters, competes in various sports-based events of the Olympics. The video game features a variety of events; some return from other video games in the series, such as boxing, soccer, swimming, and gymnastics, while including new events such as karate, skateboarding, sport climbing, and surfing.
