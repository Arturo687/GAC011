Final Fantasy VII Remake

Final Fantasy VII Remake is an action role-playing video game, published by the Square Enix company for the PlayStation 4 platform, which was released on April 10, 2020, with a temporary exclusivity of one year.

What is it about?

The remake of Final Fantasy VII, retells the story of the original video game, which follows Cloud Strife, a former Shinra soldier who joins the ecoterrorist group AVALANCHE as a mercenary to fight against the Shinra corporation, which has been draining energy. vital of the planet. Unlike the adaptations of the original video game released for computers and other high definition platforms, this is a new version completely developed from scratch, with full polygonal graphics instead of the pre-rendered environments of the original.
