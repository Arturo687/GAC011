Call of Duty Black Ops: Cold war

Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War is a first person shooter video game

What is it about?

Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War takes place after what happened in Call of Duty: Black Ops during the Cold War and is based on true events. Black Ops Cold War takes players back to the heart of the volatile geopolitical battle of the Cold War in the early 1980s. Nothing is quite what it seems in the riveting single-player campaign, where players go head-to-head with historical figures. and awkward truths as they fight around the world in iconic Cold War settings like East Berlin, Vietnam, the KGB headquarters, and more. The plot of Black Ops Cold War is also related to the stories of Call of Duty Modern Warfare and Warzone.
