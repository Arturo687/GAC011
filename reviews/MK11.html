Mortal Kombat 11

Mortal Kombat 11 (commonly abbreviated as MK11) is a fighting video game developed by NetherRealm Studios and published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment.

What is it about?

Like the previous two games in the series, "Mortal Kombat 11" is a 2.5D fighting video game. Along with the return of Fatalities and Brutalities, new gameplay features are introduced, such as Fatal Blows and Krushing Blows. The fatal blows are special moves similar to the X-ray moves in "Mortal Kombat X". Like X-ray moves, Fatal Strikes deal a large amount of damage, but unlike them, they are only available when a player's health drops below 30%, and can only be performed once per match. . Krushing Blows is a special cinematic variation of a given special move, which triggers when certain requirements are met, like most Brutalities in the previous game.
