Wii sports

Wii Sports is a sports video game, developed and produced by Nintendo as a launch title for the Wii game console.

What is it about?

The game is a five-sport simulator, designed to demonstrate the wireless and sensory capabilities of the Wii Remote to new players. The five sports included are tennis, baseball, bowling, golf, and boxing. Players use the console controller to imitate the movements made in real life in these sports, such as swinging a tennis racket. The rules of the games are simplified to make them more accessible to new players. The game also includes a training mode that monitors the player's progress in sports.
