Rocket League

Rocket League is a video game that combines soccer with vehicles. It was developed by Psyonix and released on July 7, 2015. It is available in Spanish, has cooperative, single-player and online game modes.

What is it about?

The game is similar to soccer, but using vehicles instead of players and a large ball. In the matches they can participate from 1 vs 1 to 3 vs 3 in competitive mode, in informal mode you can up to 4 vs 4 players distributed in two teams, orange and blue or red and gray when it is 2 vs 2 team against team. Each vehicle can use extra propulsion, both to accelerate and to move through the air, this being a fundamental characteristic of the game. Said propulsion can be recharged by means of dots distributed on the playing field and which continually reappear. The matches last 5 minutes, playing an indefinite extra time in case of a tie.
