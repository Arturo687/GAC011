Smite

SMITE is a third-person MOBA action video game created and published by Hi-Rez Studios for Microsoft Windows, Xbox One, PlayStation 4, and Nintendo Switch.

What is it about?

The game is based on two teams, each one made up of five gods, facing each other on a battlefield in order to destroy the enemy titan located in each of the bases. Each player puts himself in the shoes of a god from different mythologies who has different powers and characteristics. Uncontrolled characters (minions) that provide gold and experience can also be found on the battlefield.
