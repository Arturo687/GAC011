League of legends

League of Legends is a video game of the multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) and electronic sport genre.

What is it about?

The game consists of 3 current running game modes: Summoner's Rift, Howling Abyss, and Teamfight Tactics. Players compete in matches, which last between 15 and 50 minutes on average. In each game mode, teams work together to achieve a victory condition, typically destroying the central structure (called the Nexus) at the enemy team's base after bypassing a line of defensive structures called Turrets. In all game modes, players control characters called "champions," chosen or assigned in each game, who have a unique set of skills, with which they will play the entire game to its conclusion. As of April 2021, there are 155 champions available.
