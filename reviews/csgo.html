Counter Strike: Global Offensive

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive is a first-person shooter video game

What is it about?

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS: GO) is a first-person shooter video game developed by Valve Corporation in cooperation with Hidden Path Entertainment, and is the fourth game in the Counter-Strike saga, excluding Counter-Strike: Online. It was released as a beta to the market on August 21, 2012 for Microsoft Windows, Mac OS X and Xbox 360 platforms, while it suffered a delay on PlayStation 3. Subsequently, the Linux version was available to the public on September 22, 2014.
