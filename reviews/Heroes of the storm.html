Heores of the storm

Heroes of the Storm is a multiplayer online battle arena video game.

What is it about?

Heroes of the Storm is a game that revolves around a five versus five player online cooperative combat mode, operated through the Battle.net page, Blizzard's online gaming service. Players can choose from five game modes, including playing against computer-controlled heroes or against other players. When players start the game, they can only use six to ten heroes provided by the free hero rotation, a methodically selected list that changes every week, but through the use of gold, an in-game source of wealth, or Through microtransactions, you can have permanent access to a hero. There are Fifty-one heroes currently in the game divided into four different categories.
